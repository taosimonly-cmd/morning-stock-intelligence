GOAL
Put this stock app online so your computer does NOT need to stay on.

BEST FIRST HOSTING OPTION
Streamlit Community Cloud.

WHAT YOU NEED
1. A GitHub account.
2. A Streamlit Community Cloud account.
3. This folder uploaded to a new GitHub repository.

STEP BY STEP OVERVIEW
1. Go to github.com and sign in.
2. Create a new repository named: morning-stock-intelligence
3. Upload these files into the repository:
   - app.py
   - requirements.txt
   - .streamlit/config.toml
4. Go to share.streamlit.io
5. Sign in with GitHub.
6. Click New app.
7. Choose repository: morning-stock-intelligence
8. Main file path: app.py
9. Click Deploy.
10. Streamlit gives you a public web address.
11. Open that address on your phone.
12. Add it to your phone home screen.

AFTER THIS WORKS
Your computer can be turned off. Your phone will still open the app from anywhere.

IMPORTANT
This app is a research assistant, not financial advice. Signals are estimates, not guaranteed predictions.
